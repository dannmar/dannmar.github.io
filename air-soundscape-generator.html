Air Soundscape Generator

This was an accessible instrument created in collaboration with Drake Music in April 2024. The prototype featured 4 ultrasonic sensors with common sounds in the Sound Arts realm, like Ambience, Noise or Field Recordings and a FX sensor. This instrument prototype was designed for artists with disabilities, being very easy to control with any part of the body. The device was designed by Daniel Marin and Lucas Yoshimura in the London College of Communication, and it was presented at Rich Mix in Shoreditch at a Drake Music's DMLab conference.
