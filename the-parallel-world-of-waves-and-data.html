The Parallel World of Waves and Data

This audiovisual installation was exhibited at Gallery 46 in February 2024. The Parallel World of Waves and Data explores the relationship between the material and the virtual, featuring visuals and sounds generated in real time. The digital realm is breaking into our world, leaving behind a trace of physical formats and analog electronics.
