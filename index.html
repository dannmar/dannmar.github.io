Exploring Technology, Composition And Audio Experimentation

Daniel Marin is a London-based sound designer and sonic artist specializing in creating immersive sonic experiences. With a passion for crafting sonic landscapes, Daniel utilizes innovative techniques to captivate audiences and evoke emotions through sound. He has pioneered innovative techniques for spatialization, generative sound design, and multi-channel mixing. His technical mastery combined with creative vision allows him to craft sonic landscapes that envelop audiences and stir emotions.
